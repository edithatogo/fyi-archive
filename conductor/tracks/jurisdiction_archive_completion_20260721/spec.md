# Incremental jurisdiction archive completion

Issue: [fyi-archive #210](https://github.com/edithatogo/fyi-archive/issues/210). Programme: [foi-o #81](https://github.com/edithatogo/foi-o/issues/81).

For every roadmap target, accept only bounded, versioned `fyi-cli` handoffs and preserve raw public-safe requests, responses, attachments and capture metadata in immutable manifests. Record jurisdiction/profile identity, source URL, capture time, hashes, completeness, redaction/rights decisions and replay evidence.

Archive readiness does not imply capture authorization, legal completeness, NLP validity, profile promotion or publication permission.

## Acceptance

- Every target has an archived, blocked or unsupported status with evidence and no silent fallback.
- Supported increments pass integrity, replay, privacy and downstream-contract checks.
- Manifests cross-reference the exact capture adapter/version and downstream extraction/modelling run.
